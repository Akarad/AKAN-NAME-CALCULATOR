The AKAN naming style is a Ghanian tradition whereby they name you according to the day of the week you were born on. If you have never known what day it was you were born well you get to know it today together with your AKAN name. Here's a list of the names you'd get:

# DAY MALE FEMALE
1 Sunday Kwasi Akosua
2 Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
3 Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
4 Wednesday Kwaku Akua
5 Thursday Yaw Yaa
6 Friday Kofi Afua
7 Saturday Kwame Ama
